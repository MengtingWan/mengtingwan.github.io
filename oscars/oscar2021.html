Who Will Win at the Academy Awards, 2021?

Can't believe I've been doing this for five years ...

Similar to the previous years (2017, 2018, 2019, 2020), I trained a classifier to predict the winners at this year's academy awards. A couple of pre-Oscar awards were included as features. A brief summary of the results are included as below.
Feel free to check out the details and hope you're having fun in exploring this mini-project.

Who is Oscar anyway?
