I am a Senior Research Scientist at Microsoft's Office of Applied Research. Before Microsoft I was a data scientist at Airbnb working on ML and NLP problems.

I obtained my Ph.D. at University of California, San Diego (UCSD), advised by Prof. Julian McAuley. Before that, I received my master's degree in Statistics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and my undergraduate degree in Statistics from Peking University. I was also a student from the liberal arts college program (Yuanpei Program) at Peking University.

I'm interested in recommender systems, natural language processing and computational social sciences. My current research focuses on developing personalization and recommender systems for the future of productivity.
